Abstract

  • Students can write posts about - Interview Experience Preparation Tips( Study Material , Where to begin )
  • Present situation , there is not a dedicated platform for placement related discussions for SRM students sitting for placements . Students discuss in messaging chat rooms , such as WhatsApp groups. Drawback of WhatsApp group , important information can get lost in new messages and discussions are limited within friends and acquaintances.
  • These posts can be written by any students , it can be upvoted or downvoted , and commented on. After a year these posts can be viewed by coming batch for reference for their preparation.
